Latitude vs. Wing Speed

According to the plot, there is no clear correlation between latitude and wind speed. We can see a slight curve in the plot showing that cities are further from the equator may have greater wind speed. However, we do not have enough cities with this result, so this may be a coincidence and should not be assumed. We can note that there is a lower wind speed around latitudes -20 to 0 degrees. This may have some sort of cause or correlation that can be explored further.
